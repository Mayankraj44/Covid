Vaccine aapki suraksha kaise karti hai?
Vaccine aapki immunity ko badhati hai aur aapko virus se ladne mein madad karti hai
Weh virus aapke contacts mein failane ke jokhim ko bhi kam karti hai
Weh virus se infected hone par bhi aapko gambhir complications se bachne mein madad karti hai
Vaccine kaise lagwayein?
  • 1
    Ek photo ID proof ka use karte huye cowin.gov.in par register ya sign in karein
  • 2
    Ek hi mobile number ka use karte huye 4 members tak ko add karein
  • 3
    District ya PIN code ke maadhyam se vaccination centres search karein aur slot book karein
  • 4
    Vaccination centre mein register karte samay use kiya hua apna photo ya ID jarur le jayein
    • Dusron ke saath yeh suchna share karein
